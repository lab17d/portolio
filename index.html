LIS 4351: User Experience Design

Fall 2020

This course is an overview of the user experience design process. It is comprised of group design sprints in which the professor decided on an interface that the students have the challenge to redesign. At given times in the course, students also were given timed intervals on the spot to design or redesign objects based off of the lecture of the day.
